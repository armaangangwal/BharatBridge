# BharatBridge
AI-powered communication across India's language divide.
India has 22 official languages and over 19,500 dialects. Most of its people cannot fully communicate with most other people. This project is my attempt to do something about that.

WHY I'M BUILDING THIS
I grew up in Kolkata, a city of 15 million people, where Bengali, Hindi and English exist side by side and still manage to miss each other constantly.
In 2025, I hosted a student from Japan as part of an international exchange program between my school and Ogawa High School, Japan. For a week, I saw someone intelligent, curious and warm be reduced to pointing and smiling because the gap between us was measured in words neither of us had.

I started thinking, what would it take for technology to actually close that gap, not just translate words, but carry meaning across?

India's language problem is a problem of magnitude more complex. A doctor in Chennai cannot easily communicate with a patient in rural Bengal. A student in Nagpur cannpt access content in Malyalam. A grandmother in Assam cannot naviagte an application in English. These aren't edge cases. This is the norm. 

I'm building BharatBridge because I believe that language divide is one of the most solvable problems in India and also one of the most ignored in mainstream AI development.
